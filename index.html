karmatek

Welcome to the KARMATEK,the annual techfest of Govt. College of Engineering and Ceramic Technology.(GCECT)Prepare to embark on an exciting journey with KARMASHETRA, our hackathonSeveral groups of students from other colleges across West Bengal participate in search for the rich and selective knowledge provided in our workshops. In the main event, the inter-college competitions take place across the GCECT Campus. Students from GCECT as well as various other colleges await each year to show their talents in this platform consisting of thousand of students. Karmatek is all about knowledge diffusion with added thrills. For example, there is always a possibility that a participant in Karmatek can think about his opponent, "How did he even do that?"
