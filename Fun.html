Fungineering

Too much technical? Release the stress in these fun events

FACE-OFF (Debate)

Get ready to engage in the battle of wits and words and emerge victorious by proving your point! Debates are fun, informative and oh so satisfying. Participate in Face-Off for a price so less, you'll want to participate again!

UNCHARTED (Treasure Hunt)

SSHHHH! There's a treasure hidden somewhere, somewhere near, yet so far. We will give you clues but we won't lead the way, and if you are deserving enough, you will find the treasure before your rival teams do. Hurry up and get your registration done, treasure doesn't wait forever!

GRIP & RIP (Tug Of War)

Strength. Teamwork. Teamwork in Strength & Strength in Teamwork. You will need it all to win this TUG of War. We know you have brains, but do you have brawns? Register with your team and see if you can bring the other team crashing down!

EXQUIZ-IT (Quiz)

We hear that genius in you trying to outshine the others. Knowledge, you say? Weapons, we call it. Join us for an interesting general quiz, where knowledge (and timing) will be your only weapon!

ONE SHOT

How fast can you drink a bottle of soda? Fast enough to win? To burp out loud? Let's see you chug that (soft) drink down!

STRIKE OFF (Carrom)

The most Desi game to ever exist: Carrom is casually played in all clubs and gatherings. Is your finger game good enough? Try it out with us.

29 (Card)

Why should games be all about tech? Presenting the all-time classic, 29! Register with your friends to have a gala time over spades and aces.

BLITZ CUBE

Is that Rubik's cube you got as a child still lying unsolved in the bottom-most shelf of your cabinet? Maybe it's time to take it out and get things in order again. Register for our speed cubing contest, and who knows, maybe you are the next Max Park?

BEHIND-THE-LENS (Photography)

Where words end, pictures speak. Tell us your story through pictures and let everyone know the beauty that lies in your eyes!

MEME-O-MANIA (Meme Contest)

Memers, job mat choro, humare pas register karo. Don't say "Itni jaldi kya hai, kar denge", for time is ticking away! Make memes. We love dark ones too! This is an event that you must participate in (Genz ka izzat bachalo)!
